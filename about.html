OUR MISSION

PixelAddict was born from a love for retro games – the kind that defined a generation. We bring timeless classics and modern pixel-inspired creations directly to your browser. No downloads, no clutter, just instant gameplay designed for players who appreciate the charm of 8-bit and 16-bit magic.

Our games are **simple yet addictive**, perfect for a quick escape or hours of play. They aren’t just fun – they challenge your mind, sharpen your reflexes, and improve your reaction time, all while delivering that satisfying *one more round* feeling.

Whether you're reliving childhood memories or discovering pixel art gaming for the first time, PixelAddict is your home for nostalgia-fueled entertainment. Press Start and join the arcade revolution – anytime, anywhere.

About PixelAddict

We keep the arcade signal alive for every browser and every player.

PixelAddict started as a late-night experiment to beam neon-drenched cabinet games straight through the scanlines. Today we ship handcrafted releases, community events, and retro challenges—all playable free in your browser with no downloads required.

What we build

A neon pipeline of retro-first experiments

Our small team of pixel pushers, chiptune composers, and coffee-powered coders constantly prototype new riffs on classic arcade design. We ship fast, iterate faster, and keep every release tuned for modern controllers.

Weekly drops

Fresh levels, seasonal playlists, and leaderboard resets beam into the library every week.

  • Arcade, puzzle, shooter, and co-op spotlights
  • Patch notes with transparent balancing updates
  • Community-voted remasters of cult classics

Optimised for everyone

Every cabinet is tuned for keyboard, controller, and touch, with accessibility toggles for motion and colour.

  • Instant boot with no installs or plugins
  • Cross-device cloud saves on the roadmap
  • Reduced-motion and high-contrast options

Community powered

Player requests steer our release schedule and event themes—we're always listening for the next throwback.

  • Monthly livestreams with dev behind-the-scenes
  • Discord tournaments with exclusive cosmetics
  • Creator spotlights highlighting indie collabs

Signal timeline

From basement lab to browser constellation

A few highlights from our journey so far—and where we're pointed next.

  • 2019

    Prototype nights

    Two friends, an aging CRT, and an obsession with classic cabinets sparked the first PixelAddict proof-of-concept.

  • 2021

    Library goes live

    We launched the public browser hub with rotating leaderboards, challenge modes, and controller support baked in.

  • 2024 & beyond

    Expanding the grid

    Co-op raids, soundtrack drops, and an invite-only premium tier are on deck as we scale the neon universe responsibly.
